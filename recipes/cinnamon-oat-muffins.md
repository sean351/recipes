# Maple Brown Sugar Oatmeal Muffins

## Ingredients

- 2 cups all-purpose flour
- 1 cup rolled oats
- 1/2 cup brown sugar
- 2 tsp baking powder
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1/2 tsp cinnamon
- 1 cup milk
- 1/4 cup maple syrup
- 1/4 cup vegetable oil
- 1 large egg
- 1 tsp vanilla extract

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C). Line muffin tin with paper liners.
2. In a large bowl, mix flour, oats, brown sugar, baking powder, salt, and cinnamon.
3. In another bowl, whisk together milk, maple syrup, oil, egg, and vanilla.
4. Pour wet ingredients into dry ingredients, stir until just combined. Don't overmix.
5. Divide batter evenly among muffin cups, filling about 2/3 full.
6. Bake for 20-25 minutes, until golden brown and a toothpick comes out clean.
7. Cool in pan for 5 minutes, then turn out onto wire rack.

## Prep Time
15 minutes

## Cook Time
25 minutes

## Servings
12 muffins
